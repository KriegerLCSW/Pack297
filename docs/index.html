WELCOME TO CUB SCOUT PACK 297!

Welcome to Pack 297! We welcome boys and girls in Kindergarten through fifth grade.
We go camping, race cars, hike, explore nature, play games, and serve our community.
Pack meetings are on the 4th Friday each month; den meetings are every other week.
All meetings are at St. Hubert Church, 729 Grand Canyon St, Hoffman Estates, IL 60169.
Call, text, or email, and someone will respond within 48 hours!

Why Join?

  • Camping, hiking, and racing cars!
  • Family events all year round
  • Make friends and learn new skills

Pack 297 FAQ 2025 (Version 1.6, 06/17/2025)

  1. How do I get my son or daughter started?

Just scan the QR code with your phone’s camera and follow the link, or call the Cubmaster, George Krieger, at (847) 989-5056. OR JUST CLICK HERE

  1. How much does it cost to join?

Dues are $165 per year.  Of that total, $100 goes to BSA, Inc., our parent organization, and $65 goes to Pack 297.  Parents will also need to purchase a uniform for their Cub Scout.

  1. Are parents and siblings allowed to come to scout events?

Not only are parents and siblings allowed to come, they are encouraged to come!  Cub Scouts is a family organization, and we love having family involved!
