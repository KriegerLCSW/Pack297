Welcome to Pack 297!

Welcome to Pack 297! We welcome boys and girls in Kindergarten through fifth grade. We go camping, race cars, hike, explore nature, play games, and serve our community. Pack meetings are on the 4th Friday each month; den meetings are every other week. All meetings are at St. Hubert Church, 729 Grand Canyon St, Hoffman Estates, IL 60169. Call, text, or email, and someone will respond within 48 hours!

Why Join?

  • Camping, hiking, and racing cars!
  • Family events all year round
  • Make friends and learn new skills
  • Who are you?

    • I am (insert name here), a representative from Cub Scout Pack 297.  Cub Scouts are part of Scouting America, which used to be called the Boy Scouts of America.

    1. How do I get my son or daughter started?

    Just share your information with me, your Pack representative.  Someone will get back to you within 48 hours!

    1. My daughter is already in Girl Scouts. Can she be a Cub Scout too?

    Absolutely! There aren't any other activities that exclude a girl (or boy) from being a Cub Scout!

    1. Do I have to join your Pack (Pack 297)?

    Not at all! Your child is able to join any Cub Scout Pack in the world, but ours is probably closer!  Seriously, there are at least three other Cub Scout groups (called Packs) in Schaumburg Township.

    1. How much does it cost to join?

    Dues are $165 per year.  Of that total, $100 goes to BSA, Inc., our parent organization, and $65 goes to Pack 297.  The dues cover the one-year period from the initial payment, so paying on August 1st of 2025 maintains your Cub Scout’s membership until July 31st if 2025.

    1. Are parents and siblings allowed to come to scout events?

    Not only are they allowed to come, they are encouraged to come!  Cub Scouts is a family organization, and we love having family involved! We especially need parent involvement in leading our dens and joining our leadership team!

    1. What is a den? What is a pack? When do they meet?

    Each scout is assigned to a DEN based on their grade level:

    Kindergarteners are assigned to the LION DEN.

    First Graders are assigned to the TIGER DEN.

    Second Graders are assigned to the WOLF DEN.

    Third Graders are assigned to the BEAR DEN.

    Fourth Graders are assigned to the WEBELOS DEN.

    Fifth Graders are assigned to the ARROW OF LIGHT (AOL) DEN.

    DENS usually meet every other week.  All of the dens together are called a PACK.  The Pack meets monthly, plus special events.

    The majority of those meetings, with the exception of special events, take place at St. Hubert Church at 729 Grand Canyon St, Hoffman Estates, IL 60169.

    1. Are there events all year round, or just during the school year?

    We meet regularly during the year.  Over the summer, we do not have regular den or pack meetings, but we do have special events during the summer, such as swim events at Seascape Family Aquatic Center, marching in the Hoffman Estates Fourth of July Parade, and camping out overnight after the Schaumburg Boomers game on the field!

    Once school starts for the new school year, we will return to regular den and pack meetings. Most dens meet once every two weeks, and the pack meets as a whole every month.

    1. What happens after 5th grade?

    In February of 5th grade, the Cub Scouts “Crossover” to Scouts BSA.  They used to be called “Boy Scouts” At that point, they have graduated from Cub Scouts!

    1.  How can the parents of Cub Scouts get more involved?

    Cub Scouts depends on parent involvement to keep the pack running.  We are always looking for eager parents to be Den Leaders and Assistant Den Leaders,  and no experience is necessary!

    We also have a variety of other leader positions, such as Committee Chair, Treasurer, New Member Coordinator, and Advancement Coordinator.
